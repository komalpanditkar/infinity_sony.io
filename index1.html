Placement refers to the process through which students are provided with job opportunities, internships, or other career-related placements, typically through their educational institution. It's a critical part of a student's academic journey as it helps transition from education to the professional world. Placement drives are events organized by educational institutions where companies visit the campus to recruit students for job roles.
